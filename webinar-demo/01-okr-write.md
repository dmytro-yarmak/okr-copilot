# Demo 01: `$okr-write`

## Purpose

Show how Codex helps Yehor build a clean OKR from scratch in guided mode.

## Copy-Paste Prompt

```text
$okr-write

Yehor wants one team-level committed OKR for Q2 2026.
Team: Customer Success (Growth segment).
Keep language simple and practical.
Ask one question at a time.
Use Nexora context.
```

## Minimal Human Inputs

Use short answers like these as questions appear:

1. Type: `Committed`
2. Level: `Team`
3. Timeframe: `Q2 2026 (Apr 1 - Jun 30, 2026)`
4. Priority/why now: `Growth churn is 18% and low adoption is the top churn driver.`
5. Strategy link: `Lower churn supports path to profitability and stronger retention.`
6. Active Objectives count: `2 active Objectives today.`
7. Objective draft: `Increase adoption in Growth accounts so churn declines meaningfully in Q2.`
8. KR owners: `Head of Growth CS + named CSM owners by KR.`
9. Dependencies/risks: `Product usage instrumentation and limited CS capacity.`
10. Tracking/cadence: `Gainsight dashboard, weekly check-in.`

## Expected Output (Abridged)

```text
OKR Draft Complete

Objective:
Increase platform adoption in Growth accounts to reduce preventable churn in Q2 2026.

Type: Committed
Level: Team
Timeframe: Apr 1, 2026 -> Jun 30, 2026
Owner: Priya Sharma (CCO)

Key Results:
1) Raise 30-day employee activation in Growth accounts from 12% to 30% by Jun 30, 2026.
   Owner: Growth CS Lead
2) Increase completion of onboarding milestones within first 14 days from 41% to 70% by Jun 30, 2026.
   Owner: CS Ops Manager
3) Reduce annualized churn in Growth segment from 18% to 14% by Jun 30, 2026.
   Owner: Growth CSM Team Lead

Dependencies:
- Product analytics instrumentation for activation events
- CS capacity for proactive onboarding outreach

Tracking location: Gainsight + weekly CS scorecard
Check-in rhythm: Weekly
```

## Presenter Talk Track (30-45 sec)

"Write mode is guided, not automatic. Yehor keeps control, but Codex enforces quality: one clear Objective, measurable KRs, owners, and timeframe. In minutes, the draft is usable."
