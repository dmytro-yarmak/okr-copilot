## 2. CEO's 4 Strategic Bets for 2026

### Bet 1 — Path to Profitability
Reach cash-flow positive by end of Q4 2026.
- Improve gross margins 68% → 75%
- Reduce CAC payback 18 months → <15 months
- Automate onboarding and support
- Right-size cloud infrastructure (costs grew 3x vs 1.5x revenue growth)

### Bet 2 — Win the Enterprise Mid-Market
Enterprise tier (7 months old) already at 28 customers with dramatically better unit economics: 4% churn vs 18% in growth segment, ACV 3x higher. Goal: become the #1 platform for companies with 200–2,000 employees.
- Close security/compliance gaps: ISO 27001, GDPR tooling, advanced RBAC, SCIM
- Build a sales motion for larger deal sizes
- Invest in enterprise customer success

### Bet 3 — AI as Our Moat
AI skill-gap analyzer is the #1 cited win reason (73% of won deals). LearnHub investing heavily post-$80M raise. Must stay 12–18 months ahead.
- Ship AI-generated learning paths (alpha Q1, GA H1)
- Launch predictive skill readiness dashboard
- Build AI coaching assistant for managers
- Leverage data advantage that compounds over time

### Bet 4 — Make Customers Our Growth Engine
12% blended churn is too high — every churned customer must be re-acquired.
- Reduce growth segment churn (currently 18%)
- Drive expansion revenue (NRR target: 125%)
- Build referral engine and customer community

## 3. Competitive Landscape

### Primary Threat: LearnHub
- $180M raised total; $80M Series D closed Nov 2025
- ARR ~$85M | ~1,200 customers | 600 employees
- Launching "LearnHub Lite" targeting our mid-market — **direct threat**
- Win rate vs LearnHub: **52%** (down from 65% in Q3 2025)
- AI still behind ours (rules-based) but investing fast post-raise

### Secondary Competitors
| Competitor | Threat | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| SkillForge | Low | SMB-focused, moving upmarket slowly |
| Cornerstone | Low | We win 70% vs them |
| Docebo | Medium | Upper mid-market, growing AI capabilities |
| SAP SuccessFactors | Low | Only threat inside SAP ecosystems |

### Why We Win
1. AI skill-gap analyzer — "nothing else comes close" (73% of wins)
2. Speed of implementation — weeks not months (61%)
3. Modern UX (58%)
4. Customer references and NPS (42%)

### Why We Lose
1. Price pressure from budget-constrained buyers (38%)
2. Enterprise feature gaps — security, compliance, RBAC (31%)
3. Integration breadth — 45 vs LearnHub's 200+ (24%)
4. Brand recognition — "we've never heard of Nexora" (19%)

**Overall Q4 2025 win rate: 23%** (down from 28% in Q3)
