# Nexora — Company Context

> Use this fictional company as context when practicing OKR creation during the webinar.

---

## Company Overview

**Nexora** is a B2B SaaS platform that helps mid-market companies (200-2000 employees) manage their employee learning and development programs. The platform combines AI-powered skill gap analysis, personalized learning paths, and integration with external training providers.

**Founded:** 2019 in Amsterdam, Netherlands
**Headquarters:** Amsterdam, with offices in London and New York
**Employees:** 280 (up from 180 last year)
**ARR:** €24M (up from €16M last year, 50% growth)
**Customers:** 340 companies across Europe and North America
**Funding:** Series B (€35M raised in 2024)

## Market Context

**Industry:** Learning & Development Technology (L&D Tech)
**Total Addressable Market:** €12B globally
**Target Segment:** Mid-market companies (200-2000 employees)
**Primary Competitors:**
- **LearnHub** — Market leader, focused on enterprise (2000+ employees)
- **SkillForge** — Strong in SMB, weaker in mid-market
- **Traditional LMS players** — Cornerstone, SAP SuccessFactors (legacy, complex)

**Nexora's Differentiation:**
- AI-powered skill gap analysis (proprietary technology)
- Fast implementation (weeks, not months)
- Modern UX loved by both L&D teams and employees
- Strong integration ecosystem (HRIS, content providers)

**Market Trends:**
- Skills-based organizations becoming the norm
- AI disrupting learning content creation
- Increasing focus on measuring learning ROI
- Economic pressure driving efficiency in L&D spend

## Strategy 2026

### Strategic Pillars

**1. Path to Profitability**
After rapid growth, we're shifting focus from "growth at all costs" to sustainable, efficient growth. Target: achieve cash-flow positive by Q4 2026.

**2. Enterprise Expansion**
Our enterprise tier (launched 2025) is gaining traction. We need to scale our ability to land and expand in larger accounts (1000-2000 employees).

**3. AI Leadership**
Our AI skill-gap analyzer is our key differentiator. Double down on AI capabilities to widen the competitive moat.

**4. Customer Success Excellence**
Reduce churn from 12% to under 8%. Turn customers into advocates who drive referrals and case studies.

## How to Use This Context

1. **Read the company context** to understand Nexora's situation
2. **Choose a team** you want to create OKRs for
3. **Use the `/okr-review` skill** to develop team OKRs
4. **Apply personas** (CEO, Team Member, etc.) to challenge your thinking
5. **Check alignment** between your team OKRs and company OKRs

Example prompt:
```
I'm the VP of Customer Success at Nexora. Help me create OKRs that support
the company's objectives, especially around profitability and NRR.
Let's work through this as co-pilot — one question at a time.
```
