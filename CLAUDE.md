# OKR Co-pilot

This project demonstrates how to use AI as a co-pilot for OKR (Objectives and Key Results) planning and refinement.

## Philosophy

**Co-pilot, not Autopilot**: AI assists your strategic thinking but doesn't take over. You remain the driver.

- One question at a time
- Human makes all final decisions
- AI challenges, suggests, and refines - never dictates

## Skills

- `/okr-review` - Complete OKR review: refinement → quality check → alignment (one question at a time)

## Key Concepts

When refining OKRs, consider:
- **Outcome vs Output**: Focus on the change you want to see, not just deliverables
- **Leading vs Lagging indicators**: Balance predictive and results metrics
- **Alignment**: Connect team OKRs to company objectives
- **Ambition**: Stretch goals (70% achievement = success)
